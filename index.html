About

The Studio

Founded with a singular belief: that every existing structure holds untapped potential waiting to be revealed.

Studio Barnuevo is an architecture and design practice specializing in the transformation of existing spaces. Based in Spain, we work across residential, commercial, and heritage projects — each one a dialogue between what was and what could be.

Our team of architects, designers, and craftspeople shares a deep respect for built heritage and an unwavering commitment to contemporary design excellence. We don't demolish the past; we build upon it.

How We Work

Our Approach

01

Listen & Discover

Every project begins with deep listening — to the client, to the space, and to its history. We conduct thorough site analyses to understand the building's DNA before proposing any changes.

02

Envision & Design

We create a design narrative that bridges past and future. Through sketches, models, and 3D visualizations, we develop a shared vision that respects existing character while introducing contemporary clarity.

03

Craft & Build

Renovation demands exceptional craftsmanship. We work with trusted artisans and builders who share our dedication to quality, managing every detail from structural interventions to final finishes.

04

Reveal & Inhabit

The moment a transformed space is unveiled is why we do what we do. We ensure a seamless handover and remain available to support our clients as they begin their new chapter in the space.
